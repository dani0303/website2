Competiton

Botball is a competion where robots work atonmously and have to do a certain amount of task under a specific time.

We plan on having two teams this for robots, more ideas, and for the newcomers to get into robotics. There are two

competions every year, one is at Umass Lowell where we compete against schools in our state. The other will be at

St. Augustine where will be competing against others schools internationally. We hope by forming a second team we

the new comers will have a good idea of how our team works and learn the basics of programming and the mechanics of

robotics.
